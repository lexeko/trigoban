How It Works

Each tile is one equilateral triangle, and triangles alternate orientation. In play you can move or push horizontally, diagonally along the two slanted axes, and vertically through the top or bottom edge when the current triangle orientation allows it.

Progress is saved automatically in your browser, including the current board, counters, and undo history for each level.
